welcome to klean

who is klean

klean... a state of mind and how i see things

I am a lover of all things beautiful. Images, words, sounds, ideas. With that inspiration, I create. The seed for my creative spirit was planted when I was eight years old, watching my grandfather transform a blank canvas into a landscape. As I watched the canvas fill with grass, flowers and cattle, I realized that beautiful things weren’t just meant to be passively observed; I could create them as well.


I was hooked instantly, and I’ve never looked back, moving from paper and pencil to mouse and monitor. I expanded into coding and programming so I could provide my clients with a more well-rounded offering of services. My design aesthetic is about crisp lines and sharp colors – in short, it’s klean. Each client represents the opportunity for mutual growth and inspiration. Life never ceases to surprise me, and I find that being open and collaborative is the best way to serve my clients.


Outside of designing and coding, I am a poet, retired spoken word artist, and sneaker fanatic, I have an unhealthy affinity for Diet Dr. Pepper, and Pretty Woman is one of my favorite movies. As you can see, I don't really fit inside any boxes; my versatility and communication skills are what set me apart. That, and the fact that I get to nurture my creative spirit by rediscovering the world through the eyes of my two amazing sons, Jah, Zion and their little princess of a sister Zoe.

ascend client solutions

a rebranding mission

Rebranding a company can be an arduous task, yet it can breathe new life and evoke a newenergy into a company who's image and/or sales are suffering. I was tasked with rebranding Ascend Client Solutions, formerly known as Salesdrivers and I was more than up to the task. From the name change to a new direction and identity I was responsible for creating it all. The name Ascend more closely related to the work they wanted to achieve, the identity/logo depicts an abstract stairway "ascending" without being a literal interpretation. I was then tasked to create a branding guide and several other pieces of collateral to announce to the world that Ascend is here.

  • Date: 2012
  • Client: Ascend
  • Category: graphic design

edge-group.com

website re-design

Edge Marketing Procurement Inc., hired me as a graphic design assistant. They weren't aware of my web development ability until they needed to redesign their website and to make it one of the best in the industry. After watching a painful process of dealing with developers and web design firms, I was asked to step up to the plate after I had redesigned the mockups for one web design firm. I was excited about the challenge and more importantly the opportunity. I had three weeks a blank canvas and a desire to make Edge's new site my calling card in the industry. This wasn't an easy task. Edge has been in business for 23 years and have a reputation quality and innovation. I had to take in consideration that their member base, both current and potential, are not always going to be the most web savvy. I also had to create a site that would leave room for growth. I will soon be tasked with creating a similar look and feel for their web portal and intranet, projects I am looking forward to. In a matter of months, I went from graphic design assistant to Senior Designer, a role in which I cherish./p>

  • Date: 2012
  • Client: the edge group
  • Category: web design
